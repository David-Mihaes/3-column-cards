SPORT

A sports car is a type of car that is designed with an emphasis on dynamic performance, such as handling, acceleration, top speed, the thrill of driving, and racing capability.

LUXURY

A luxury car is a car that provides above-average to high-end levels of comfort, features, and equipment. Often, more expensive materials and surface finishes are used, and buyers expect better build quality.

UTILITY

A sport utility vehicle is a car classification that combines elements of road-going passenger cars with features from off-road vehicles, such as raised ground clearance and four-wheel drive.

SPORT

A sport bike is a motorcycle designed and optimized for speed, acceleration, braking, and cornering on asphalt concrete race tracks and roads, mainly designed for performance at the expense of comfort, fuel economy, safety, noise reduction and storage in comparison with other motorcycles.

CRUISER

A cruiser motorcycle is a motorcycle where riding position usually places the feet forward and the hands up, with the spine erect or leaning back slightly, emphasize easy rideability and shifting, with plenty of low-end torque but not necessarily large amounts of horsepower.

ADVENTURE

A dual-sport motorcycle is a type of motorcycle that is designed for varying degrees of off-road use while still being street-legal, equipped with lights, a speedometer, mirrors, a horn, registration plates, and a muffler to comply with local regulations.
